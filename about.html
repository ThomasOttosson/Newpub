Our Story


Mr Allen Anderson founded The Anderson Club in 1877 since he was a retired succesful businessman who was eager to give back to the community and help new entrepreneurs grow their career.

Mr Anderson has done plenty of deals and been a majority shareholder of several businesses during his career but he was mostly recognized for discovering oil in the Atlantic Ocean through is company called: Anderson Investment Consortium Ltd which saved England from bankruptcy in 1861. As a result of Mr Anderson's accomplishment he recived an award from the royal family in the UK and became a member of the House of Lords.

Mr Anderson died in 1889 and The Anderson Club is currently being runned by his grandchild Neil Anderson. Neil Anderson has inherited all of Mr Anderson's Trust funds and has no plans to stop. Neil's goal is to continue to manage and grow Mr Anderson's legacy, inspire young people and help people who are in need through his trust fund called: First Aid Help UK trust fund.
